]]> Gronau-Epe helium storage site, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany https://www.vinci-construction.com/en/our-solutions-and-services/oil-gas/gronau-epe-helium-storage-site-north-rhine-westphalia-germany/ Wed, 10 Feb 2021 13:45:59 +0000 https://www.vinci-construction.com/?post_type=nos_expertises&p=3004 With the inauguration of the first commercial pure helium storage site in September 2016, Air Liquide provided a solution to the main issue facing the market for the noble gas: rapidly responding to customer demand. The challenge was to guarantee delivery times to customer sites, avoid losses due to helium heating during transport and secure the supply of helium *via *an efficient logistics system.

The challenge has now been met with the new storage site in Gronau-Epe, North Rhine-Westphalia, some 120 km from Düsseldorf. Helium is stored in large quantities at a depth of 1,300 metres in a salt cavern and the natural brine is used to adjust the storage volume. For UGS GmbH, a subsidiary of Geostock Holding (Entrepose Group), the adventure took place between November 2015 and July 2016. The challenge was to design a new specific helium storage shaft. The result is commensurate with the goal. The new site gives Air Liquide, world leader in helium since its acquisition of Airgas, a more reliable and more predictable supply system.

]]> Dunkirk LNG, Dunkirk, France https://www.vinci-construction.com/en/our-solutions-and-services/oil-gas/dunkirk-lng-dunkirk-france/ Wed, 10 Feb 2021 12:51:02 +0000 https://www.vinci-construction.com/?post_type=nos_expertises&p=2985 In response to the need for greater safety and more diverse means of energy supply in Europe, EDF launched the construction of a methane terminal at the Port of Dunkirk; the project will also increase liquefied natural gas (LNG) import capacity.

Entrepose Contracting built the three 190,000 cu. metre LNG storage tanks on a turnkey basis. They are among the world’s largest cryogenic storage tanks. Each full integrity tank has an inner steel (9% nickel) hull, which maintains LNG at a temperature of -163°C.

Several VINCI Construction subsidiaries pooled their complementary expertise to build a variety of structures at the site: a BOG (Boil-off Gas) building, a pumping station (Seawater Intake), two discharge tanks, a Qmax tanker jetty and an undersea tunnel to carry warm water from the Gravelines nuclear power station to the plant, and to carry out soil improvement works.

]]> LNG tanks, Wheatstone, Australia https://www.vinci-construction.com/en/our-solutions-and-services/oil-gas/lng-tanks-wheatstone-australia/ Wed, 10 Feb 2021 12:26:33 +0000 https://www.vinci-construction.com/?post_type=nos_expertises&p=2968 VINCI Construction Grands Projets, Entrepose Contracting, Menard Bachy and Australia’s Thiess company built four LNG (liquefied natural gas) tanks in the desert of northwest Australia under an EPC (Engineering, Procurement, Construction) contract. The turnkey project is part of one of the country’s largest natural resource development programmes. It comprises creation of two double-walled LNG tanks with a unit storage capacity of 150,000 cu. metres and two condensate storage tanks with a unit capacity of 120,000 cu. metres. The roofs were airlifted onto the tanks.

The project, carried out in an extreme environment 1,500 km from the nearest urban centre, was completed in November 2016 after 4 million hours of works.
