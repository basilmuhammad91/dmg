DMG Construction Group has more than 15 years of experience in the construction industry. We have been operating in Canada for over 10 years providing services in 8 different construction sectors. By providing high-quality training to our workforce and building long-term relationships with them, our projects are staffed with highly trained professionals working at all levels of project execution.




Building

DMG Construction Group is specialized in building Aviation, Arts & Culture, federal, Higher Education, Health Care, Government, Hospitality, Transport, and Entertainment projects.


Industrial

DMG Construction Group provides a variety of innovative solutions in the Industrial market including food and beverage, industrial water, industrial energy, metals, mineral processing, process industries, pulp and paper, and specialty chemicals.


Oil & Gas

We have successfully delivered more than 1000 projects in Oil and Gas market over the last 15 years by combining a direct-hire self-perform model with meticulous front-end involvement, design, and planning.


Mining

DMG Construction Group is blessed to have some of the most highly skilled professionals in the Mining Industry. We are specialized in Mine Management, Mine Production, Infrastructure Construction, and maintenance.


Power

The Power Market is continuously growing and evolving; offering new opportunities for power Generation, Storage, and Distribution.


Transportation

DMG Construction has one of the largest privately-owned fleet of construction equipment in Canada which enables the company to rapidly mobilize the necessary resources for any project.

OUR WORK

We manage complex projects, setting a benchmark through our expertise in soil, structures, nuclear engineering and digital technology for construction. Our experts offer a full range of general contracting services, specializing in mechanical, civil and electrical construction, as well as piping and plant shutdowns/turnarounds.

Environment

Construction offers turnkey solutions for protecting the environment: design-build construction of water, waste and flue gas treatment facilities that combine civil and process engineering, soil remediation expertise, asbestos removal and polluted site deconstruction.

Soil decontamination

Douaumont and Vaux forts, Meuse – Moselle, France

Nearly a century after the end of hostilities in World War I, the pyrotechnic soil cleanup in eastern France is still ongoing and crucial to restoring a natural area.

Soil decontamination

Hydromorphological restoration of the Glandon riverbed at Maurienne (France)

Led by the teams of Construction Terrassement in Bourgneuf in conjunction with Equo Vivo, the hydromorphological restoration of the Glandon river was designed to diversify its flows, create new habitats, improve water quality and restore biodiversity in this tributary of the Isère.

Deconstruction

Condé sur Noireau factory, Calvados, France

Deconstruction of a former factory to clean up the building prior to demolition.

Water infrastructure

Building on its expertise dating back more than 120 years, Construction has developed comprehensive capabilities covering the entire water cycle, including standout expertise in areas such as drinking water pumping and supply, wastewater treatment and discharge, rainwater management and dam design, construction and maintenance.

Canalisations

PS70 pumping station, Doha, Qatar

With the occurrence of particularly strong storms regularly leading to the closure of the Cortiou inlet, construction of an emergency tank was crucial for the region.

Dams

Hydroelectric dam, Assiut, Egypt

Dams on the Nile, designed to generate electricity, irrigate fields and ensure shipping on the iconic river, remain outsized undertakings.

Canalisations

Water distribution network, Abidjan, Côte d’Ivoire

Work to reinforce the water distribution system is designed to expand access to drinking water for local residents regularly faced with water shortages due to lack of infrastructure investment and maintenance, and to improve the continuity and quality of the urban water supply.
