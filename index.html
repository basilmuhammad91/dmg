Our operations generate waste. We wanted to take action and change things.

18 March, 2021

S2R, a Construction subsidiary on Reunion Island, is carrying out building decommissioning and refurbishment projects. The manager, Faouzi Ben-Abdallah, has for a long time been looking for better ways to manage the waste generated by this type of operation. Over the past three years, the company has made progress in examining various solutions before going forward, with very encouraging results and prospects.

Chernobyl, the ultimate challenge

15 March, 2021

Ten years ago, construction works began on a gigantic arch-shaped structure to confine Chernobyl power plant’s reactor number 4, which was damaged in an explosion in 1986. Here’s more about a once-in-a-lifetime project that showcases Construction’s wealth of expertise.

Strong mobilization and construction of hospitals in the UK

3 March, 2021

Teams from Integrated Health Projects (IHP) – a joint venture between Construction UK and Sir Robert McAlpine – successfully converted Manchester Central Convention Complex into a 750-bed temporary hospital in under two weeks in spring 2020.

After 2 years of research and confidential preparation, a group of 30 pioneering European energy players officially launches “HyDeal Ambition” with the aim of delivering 100% green hydrogen across Europe at €1.5/kg before 2030.

Flashback to 1998: the Vasco de Gama bridge in Lisbon is inaugurated.

10 February, 2021

Inaugurated at the Lisbon Universal Exhibition in 1998, the Vasco de Gama Bridge is remarkable for several reasons. Seventeen kilometres long, it spans the Tagus estuary and for many years remained the longest bridge in Europe.
